TL;DR

Support will be the main hub and take primary responsibility for ongoing support of clients. The goal is to provide a system to keep track of all client interactions with each department and have support coordinate efforts when other departments are required to solve the problem.

A client only needs to contact one person in Customer Success for us to take care of their need. Support will handle tasks for pre-existing clients (those out of Implementations or not paying for additional services) and handle or delegate tasks as needed.
